## What is this?

Most hackathon registrations are just a Google Form.  
This isn't that.

NexusHack 2026 is a full-stack event platform built for KLS Gogte Institute of Technology — , real-time database, and a confirmation system that makes participants feel like they just joined something big.

---

## What can it do?

- ⏱️ Live countdown to event day
- 📋 Registration form with real-time validation
- 🔑 Auto-generates unique ID per participant — `NXH-2026-XXXX-XXXX`
- 🚫 Prevents duplicate registrations
- 📱 Works on all screen sizes
